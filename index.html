The
Coffee House

Established 1984

The Coffee House is perfectly located in the heart of Royal Leamington Spa, with a beautiful tree lined view on Regent Grove. Celebrating the art of simplicity, enjoy a drink, a bite to eat and some time to recharge in a relaxed, comfortable and friendly atmosphere. Step in and set all distractions aside. Let us become your third space, your place to connect with yourself and others.

Menu

As well as our specially brewed coffee, we also offer a large range of wine, local craft beers and other alcoholic beverages. Our menu is always changing so pop in to see our most up-to-date options, including a wide variety of vegan and gluten free food.
